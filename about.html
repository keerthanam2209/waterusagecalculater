Who We Are

We are a team of passionate engineering students committed to promoting water conservation and sustainability. Our project focuses on spreading awareness about the concept of the water footprint â€” the hidden amount of water used in producing goods and services we use daily.

Our Mission

To inspire individuals, institutions, and industries to take responsible actions that reduce unnecessary water consumption and protect this vital resource for future generations.

Our Goals

  • Raise awareness about direct and indirect water consumption.
  • Encourage efficient water management in homes, schools, and industries.
  • Promote innovative solutions for sustainable water use.
  • Foster a culture of conservation among youth and communities.

Our Approach

We believe in combining technology, education, and collaboration. Through workshops, research, and outreach programs, we aim to engage students and communities in creating a meaningful impact toward reducing their water footprint.
